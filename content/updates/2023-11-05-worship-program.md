# 信息摘要

Preaching Philippians “Follow Jesus with Joy”
宣讲《腓利比书》“喜乐跟随主”

喜乐的生命 The Joyful Life
腓立比书 (Philippians) 4: 1-7
主旨 Thesis
喜乐是我的态度 Joy is my attitude

• 靠主站立得稳 Stand firm in the Lord (6: 1)
• 在主里同心 Be the same mind in the Lord (6: 2-3)
• 靠主常常喜乐 Rejoice in the Lord always (6: 4)
• 彰显谦让之心 Let your gentleness be seen (6: 5)
• 应当一无挂虑 Do not worry about anything (6: 6-7)
     1) 不要挂虑 Do not worry
      2) 告诉神 Present requests to God
      3) 应许 Promise

结语Conclusion
愿赐平安的神保守我们在主里一直喜乐！
May the peace of God guard us to be joyful always in the Lord!
