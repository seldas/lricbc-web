# 信息摘要

以基督耶稣的心为心
腓立比书 (Philippians) 2: 1-11
Have the Same Mind of Christ Jesus

1. 要同心 Be the same mind (2:2)
2. 不结党、不虚荣 No self-ambition or vanity (2:3a)
3. 看别人比自己强 Consider others better than yourself (2:3b)
4. 不单顾及自己、也顾及别人
Do not look only to your own interest, but to others’ interest (2:4)
5. 要以基督耶稣的心为心 Have the same mind of Christ Jesus (2:5)

基督耶稣的心 The mind of Jesus (2:5)

1. 不强夺 Do not grasp tightly (2:6)
2. 虚己 Empty yourself (2:7)
3. 卑微 Be humble (2:8)
4. 顺服 Be obedient (2:9)

结语 Conclusion
让我们每日操练有基督的心、有基督的眼、
基督的手、基督的脚！
Let us exercise to have Jesus’ heart, Jesus’ eyes,
Jesus’ hands, and Jesus’ feet daily!
