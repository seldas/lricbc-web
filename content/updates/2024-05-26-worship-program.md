# 信息摘要

Time For a Change  是改变的时候了
																					马可福音 (Mark) 5: 25-34 ; 10: 46-52

要做改变之前, 你先要:
																		In order to make a change, you need to:

1.  不要滿足于現状
																		Stop Settling for the Way things Are

2.  不要再等待理想的環境
																		Stop Waiting for Ideal Circumstances

3.  不要再听別人说的话
																		Stop Listening to What Other People Say

4.  开始走向唯一有能力改变你的人 -- 耶穌
																		Start Moving Toward the Only One Who Has The Power to Change You – Jesus

我们都需要凭著信心来到耶穌面前,
																		只有衪能永运改变我们的生命!   
																		We all need to come to Jesus by faith, 
																		only He can change our lives forever
