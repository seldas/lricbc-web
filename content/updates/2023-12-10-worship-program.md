# 信息摘要

“耶稣带来什么”系列信息之二
						A Series of Messages: “What Does Jesus Bring?” (2)

耶稣带来医治 Jesus Brings Healing
			 马可福音 (Mark) 5: 1-20

主旨 Thesis 
			  认识主医治的大能，见证主复活的生命

1. 捆绑中的痛苦Suffering in Bondage (5:1-5)

a. 坟茔The tombs
			b. 失控Out of control
			c. 自我伤害Self-abused

2. 神儿子的权柄Authority of the Son of God (5: 6-13)
			 a. 鬼认出主 The devil knows the Lord
			 b. 主吩咐鬼 The Lord commands the devil
			 c. 鬼的名字 The name of the devil
			 d. 鬼入猪群 The devil goes into pigs

3. 释放后的自由Freedom After Release (5: 15) 
			a. 坐着 Sitting
			b. 穿上衣服 Dressed 
			c. 心里明白 In his right mind

4. 城里人的回应Response of the People in the City (5: 14-17)

结语 Conclusion
			  去将福音传给你的家人！
			 Go and share the Good News with your family!
