## Message 信息

**A Mother of Faith: Hannah / 信心的母亲：哈拿**  
*Pastor Li Chunhai (李春海牧师)*

**Thesis 主旨**

Live by faith! 活在信心里！

* Prayer in Distress 苦境中的祈祷 (1 Samuel 1:1–20)
* Faithful Fulfillment of a Vow 甘心的还愿 (1:21–28)
* Joyful Service 喜乐的事奉 (2:1–10)

**Challenge 挑战**

Entrust what you treasure most — your child, your future, and all that you find hardest to let go of — to the Lord.  
把你最看重的交给主——你的孩子、你的未来、以及你最放不下的一切。

*Scripture Memory: 1 Samuel 2:1 / 背诵经文：撒母耳记上 2:1*

> "Then Hannah prayed and said: 'My heart rejoices in the Lord; in the Lord my horn is lifted high. My mouth boasts over my enemies, for I delight in your deliverance.'"  
> "哈拿祷告说：我的心因耶和华快乐；我的角因耶和华高举。我的口向仇敌张开；我因耶和华的救恩欢欣。"
