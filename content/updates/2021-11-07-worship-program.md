# 信息摘要

新约The New Covenant
（希伯来书 (Hebrews) 8: 6-13）

主立新约  The Lord made a New Covenant
(太Matthew 26: 28；可Mark 14: 24；路Luke 22: 20)

新约是完美之约  The New Covenant is the Perfect Covenant (来 8: 6-13)
        1.  完美的中保  The Perfect Mediator (8: 6)
        2.  律法写在心上  The Law is written in the heart (8: 10)

在新约中更新我与主的关系 
Renew my relationship with the Lord in the New Covenant

主餐礼是教会的礼仪。弟兄姐妹聚集在一起记念主，思想祂的受死、庆祝祂的复活，盼望祂的再来。
The Lord’s Supper is a church ordinance. Brothers and sisters gather together to remember our Lord, meditating His death, celebrating His resurrection, and hoping for His second coming.        
——省察自己Examine Ourselves
