# 信息摘要

吹起福音的号角Blow the Gospel Trumpet
 民数记 (Numbers) 10: 1-10

1. 制作号角Make Trumpets（民Nu 10:1-2）
2. 吹号手Trumpeters (10: 8)
3. 用途Instructions for Use 
a.  召集Calling together (10: 3-4)：百姓The community /领袖 Leaders  
b.  起行Having the camps set out (10: 5-6)
c.  争战Battles (10: 9)
d.  庆祝Celebrations (10: 10)

挑战：我们需要更多吹号手We need more trumpeters
 我们需要听到号角声We need to hear the sound of trumpet

愿上帝祝福我们，在这个时代中为主吹起福音的号角！
May God bless us to blow the gospel trumpet for the Lord in this age!
