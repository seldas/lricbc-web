# 信息摘要

向主交帐 Give Account to God
    马太福音 (Matthew) 25: 14-30

才干的比喻 The Parable of the Talents 
	比喻的几个步骤 A Few Steps of the Parable 
	1. 受主人的信托 Be entrusted by the master (25: 14-15)
	2. 对待信托的两种做法 Two approaches of treating entrustment (25: 16-18)
	3. 主人回来 The master comes back (25: 19)
	4. 主人与仆人算帐 The master settles account with the servants

a. 交帐与赏赐 Account and reward (25: 20-23)
          b. 交帐与受罚Account and punishment (25:24-30)

挑战 Challenge
    向主悔改 Rrepent to God

结语 Conclusion
活出上帝的生命，作出上帝的工作！
Live out God’s life, do God’s work!
