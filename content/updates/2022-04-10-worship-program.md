# 信息摘要

主餐礼宣告福音
     Declare the Good News in the Lord’s Supper
     哥林多前书 (1 Corinthians) 11: 23-29

•    “表明” ：公开宣告declare openly, 宣布proclaim, 宣讲preach, 赞美laud,  庆祝celebrate  (林前1 Co 11: 26)

•    这是主的吩咐This is the Lord’s Command (林前 1 Co 11: 23, 24, 25)

1. 在教会聚会中宣告Declare it in Church Gathering  (林前1 Co 11: 17, 18, 20, 22, 33)
2. 在教会合一中宣告  Declare it in Church Unity (林前 1 Co 10:17)

a. 求同存异、彼此相愛 (约Jn 13: 34)
Seek common ground while reserving differences, love each other

b. 同一心灵、同一脚踪 (林后2 Co 12: 18)
 The same spirit , the same course

3. 在顺服的行动中宣告 Declare it in the Act of Obedience (林前1 Co 11: 23, 24)

•    结语 Conclusion：直等到祂来！Until He comes!

請將奉獻放進小禮堂後面的奉獻盒子
