**7/7/24 Pastor’s Word**
Learning “what” to Remember

“In Remembrance of Me”

When we see God as He truly is, we will worship Him in both a first love passion and reverential fear. Make no mistake—both are essential.

The Lord’s Supper is a Command (Not an “Option” Believers Can Ignore). In Greek, “do this” definitely are in the form of a command. There is something uniquely powerful about this awesome remembrance. Received in faith, the Lord’s Supper strengthens us in our covenant, renews our focus and nourishes our spirit. It is a command of phenomenal benefit to believers and vital to fully abiding in Christ.

First Remembrance--- The Lord’s Supper Symbolizes the Unequalled Sacrifice of Christ for Mankind (Luke 22:19)

•    A cost beyond measure (Isaiah 53:6). When partaking of the Lord’s Supper, Jesus specifically commands us to “remember His broken body and blood on our behalf.” The key point to remember is the unspeakable cost God paid to forgive our sins

•    The honor of the cross reflects the horror of sin (Hebrews 12: 26)

•    The Lord’s supper reminds of our call to live holy recognizing that our sins still grieve God

•    We should always remember “why” he came the passion for missions and God’s glory

•    We are reminded that Jesus is coming back there will be a final great wedding feast

•    We love him because he first loved us so we deny ourselves and take up our cross

Second Remembrance --- The Lord’s Supper Reflects the “New Covenant” in Christ’s Blood (Luke 22: 20)

•    The glory of the “New Covenant” why it is so superior to the old

•    The “New Covenant” means believers have the power to know God deeply and live victoriously(John 1: 12; Romans 6: 14; 2 Corinthians 2: 14)

•    No excuses for continuing in bondage to sin (Romans 6:14; 1 Corinthians 10:13)

Third Remembrance --- The Lord’s Supper Reminds of Christ’s Desire for Believers to be United in Love (John 13: 34-35, 17: 20-22)

•    Open ungodliness and disunity is a dangerous profaning of the Lord’s supper

•    Lord’s Suppers are to be a celebration of unity; it is the early church love feast(1 Corinthians 11: 17-22; Jude 12)

The Lord’s Supper should remind that our unity is far greater than issues such as organizational polity, generational preferences or music style. To take the Lord’s Supper while engaged in serious unyielded anger, rancor and division is near blasphemy.

•    A most crucial time to come together; “put away all wrath and evil speaking”(Ephesians 4: 29-32)

Fourth Remembrance --- The Lord’s Supper Vividly Symbolizes God’s Covenant of Grace and Love (John 3: 16; Ephesians 2: 8-9)

•    A symbol of unequalled remembrance to God Himself—the incredible meaning to the Father and Son

•    Above all --- we celebrate God’s grace and love he longs for us to come to His table (Luke 22:15)

By no means must we be perfect to take the Lord’s Supper. Our Father knows we are all a work in progress(Philippians 3: 12-14). Yes wonder of all wonders, Jesus said He “earnestly desired” to conduct the Passover meal and Lord’s Supper with disciples He knew were going to betray Him(Luke 22:15)! Believers in spite of our failures, Jesus earnestly desires the observance with us as well. Remember, the Lord fully accepts us by His blood and grace (Ephesians 1:6).

Confess all sin and trust God for a spirit of full love and surrender. Remember that His grace is sufficient! (1 Corinthians 10: 13; 2 Corinthians 12:9). Because God has so loved you, commit all your love to Him.

学习记念“什么”

“记念我”

当我们真正认识上帝时，我们会怀着起初的愛心和敬畏之心来崇拜他。毫无疑问—两者都是必不可少的！

主餐是命令（不是信徒可以忽略的“选择” ）。希腊文中的“如此行” 肯定是以命令的形式出现的。这种令人敬畏的记念具有独特的力量。凭着信心领受主餐，我们可以坚定我们与主的盟约，更新我们的聚焦，并滋养我们的灵性。这是一项对信徒有极大益处的命令，对于完全住在基督里至关重要。

### 第一个记念—主餐象征着基督为人类作出的无与伦比的牺牲（路加福音22:19 ）

•    无法估量的代价（以赛亚书 53:6）。当领受主餐时，耶稣特别命令我们“记念他为我们舍身流血” 。 要记住的关键点是，上帝为赦免我们的罪付出了难以言喻的代价

### •    十字架的荣耀反映出罪的恐怖（希伯来书12:26）

### •    主餐提醒我们要活出圣洁，认识到我们的罪仍然令上帝悲伤

### •    我们应该永远记住基督为什么来到世界，基督是为了使命和上帝的荣耀

### •    主餐提醒我们，耶稣将会再来，那时将会有一场最后的盛大婚宴

### •    我们愛他，因为他先愛我们，所以我们舍己，背起自己的十字架跟随他

### 第二个记念—主的晚餐反映出基督宝血的“新约” (路加福音22:20)

### •    “新约”的荣耀；新约远超过旧约

•    “新约”意味着信徒有能力深刻认识神并活出得胜的生活（约翰福音1:12；罗马书 6:14；哥林多后书2:14）

•    我们没有任何借口可以继续被罪所束缚（罗马书 6:14；哥林多前书 10:13）

第三个记念—主餐提醒我们基督盼望信徒在愛中合而为一（约翰福音13:34-35 ;

### •    不敬虔和不合一是对主餐的危险亵渎

•    主餐是合一的庆祝，是早期教会的愛筵（哥林多前书11:17-22；犹大书12）

主餐应该提醒我们：合一远大于组织体制、代沟偏好或音乐风格等问题。在极度愤怒、怨恨和分裂的情况下领受主餐，几乎是对上帝的亵渎。

•    这是聚集在一起的最重要时刻； “除掉一切愤怒和毁谤” （以弗所书 4: 29-32 ）

第四个记念—主餐生动地象征着上帝恩典和慈愛的盟约（约翰福音3:16；以弗所书2:8-9 ）

### •    无与伦比的记念上帝的象征—对圣父和圣子不可思议的意义

•    重要的是，我们庆祝上帝的恩典和慈愛，他渴望我们来到他的餐桌前（路加福音 22:15 )

这并不是说我们完全了才能领受主餐。我们的天父知道我们都在不断进步（腓立比书 3: 12-14）。是的，奇妙中的奇妙，耶稣说他“很愿意”与他知道将要背叛他的门徒一起吃逾越节晚餐和主的晚餐（路加福音 22: 15）！信徒，尽管我们经常失败，耶稣也热切地希望我们一起守主餐。记住，主通过他的血和恩典完全接受我们（以弗所书 1: 6）。

承认一切罪孽，相信上帝会赐予你全心全意的愛和顺服。记住，他的恩典够你用的！（哥林多前书 10:13；哥林多后书 12:9）。因为上帝如此愛你，所以把你所有的愛都献给他吧！
