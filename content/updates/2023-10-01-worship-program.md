# 信息摘要

明光照耀 Shining Like Stars
  腓立比书 (Philippians) 2: 12-18

1. 照耀的前提 Premise of Shining (2: 12-14)
a. 作成得救的功夫 Work out your salvation
b. 不要发怨言、起争竞 Do not murmur or argue

2. 照耀的见证 Testimony of Shining (2: 15-16)
a. 神的儿女 God’s children
b. 生命的道 The word of life

3. 照耀的果效 Effect of Shining (2: 17-18)
a. 献祭 Sacrifice
b. 喜乐 Joy

结语 Conclusion
求主带领我们每日都将信心的祭物献给主！阿门！
May the Lord lead us to offer the sacrifice of faith to the Lord
every day! Amen!
