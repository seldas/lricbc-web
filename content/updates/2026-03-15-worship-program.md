### 信息摘要
### “神人同住”——宣讲会幕之二
“God is with us”: Preaching on the Tabernacle (II)  
### 主的工人 The Lord’s Workers
诵读经文 Scripture Reading: 出(Exo) 31:1-11 ; 35:30-36:2 ; 38:22-23  
参考经文 Reference: 出(Exo) 35:10; 36:8; 37:1; 39:1; 代上(1 Chro) 2:20; 代下(2 Chor) 1:5  
### 主旨 Thesis
### 主的心意在于人!（弗2:10）

> **Scripture:** The Lord’s heart is on man! (Eph 2:10)
### •    在描述中工人的出场次序 The Order of Workers in Narrative
(31:1-18; 35:1-36:3)  
### •    工人们 Workers (31:1-6 ; 35:10 ; 35:30-36: 2 ; 38:22-23)
* **1.** 被呼召 Called by God (31:2, 6; 35:10; 36:2)
* **2.** 圣灵充满与恩赐 Filled with the Spirit and endowed with gifts
(31:3-6; 31:34; 38:23)  
* **3.** 具体工作 Specific tasks (31:7-11)
### •    我们的服侍 Our service
* **1.** 上帝___________工人God ___________ workers (太Matt 4:19)
* **2.** 上帝________工人 God ________ workers (徒Acts 1:8; 弗Eph 4:11-12)
* **3.** 上帝开工也必完工 God begins his work and will finish it (腓Phi 1:6)
### 结语 Conclusion
### 来吧！与主同行、与主同工！(歌2:10-13)

> **Scripture:** Come, walk with the Lord, and work with the Lord. (Songs 2:10-13)
### 背诵经文 Scripture Memory: 以弗所书 (Eph) 2:10
### “我们原是祂的工作，在基督耶稣里造成的，为要叫我们行善，就是
### 神所预备叫我们行的。”
“For we are God’s workmanship, created in Christ Jesus to do  
good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do.”
