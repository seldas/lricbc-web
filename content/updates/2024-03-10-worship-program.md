# 信息摘要

“更深亲近上帝”：诗篇选讲系列之九
												“Be Closer to God”: Preaching Selections from the Psalms IX

上行之诗前三篇  The First Three Psalms of Ascents
												诗篇Psalms 120, 121, 122

主旨Theme
												    在天路上，克服重重困难，通向得胜与荣耀
												    On the road to heaven, overcome many difficulties and 
												     move toward victory and glory

第一步：求脱离口舌之祸 (120)
												         The First Step: Asking freedom from the evil of words (120)

废弃有祸之舌 Abandon the tongue of woe
													离开有祸之地 Leave the place of trouble

第二步：求个人的保护 (121)
												         The Second Step: Asking Personal Protection

保护者 Protector (1-3)
													全方位的保护 Comprehensive Protection (4-8)

第三步：求教会的平安 (122)
												         The Third Step: Seeking the Peace of the Church

去敬拜 Go to Worship (1)
													立场 Standpoint (2)
													教会生活 Church Life (3-5)
													祝福 Blessings (6-9)

结语Conclusion
												但义人的路好像黎明的光，越照越明，直到日午 (箴4:18)。
												But the path of the just is as the shining light, that shineth more and more 
												unto the perfect day (Proverbs 4:18).
