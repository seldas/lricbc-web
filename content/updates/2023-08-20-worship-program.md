# 信息摘要

神的荣耀 God’s Glory   
 哈该书(Haggai) 2: 1-9

主旨 Thesis
     为主的荣耀刚强作工，主必供应一切
     Be strong and work for the Lord’s glory, and He will provide everything

先知说了三件事 The prophet spoke three things
1. 主鼓励他们刚强做工 (2: 3-5)
The Lord encouraged them to be strong and work

2. 主保障财宝的供应 (2: 6-7)
The Lord guaranteed the provision of treasure

3. 主宣告主权和应许 (2: 8-9)
The Lord claimed sovereignty and promise

给我们的信息The message for us

结语 Conclusion
愿上帝的荣耀彰显在我们中间！
May the glory of the Lord demonstrate among us!
