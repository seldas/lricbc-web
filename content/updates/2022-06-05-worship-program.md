# 信息摘要

窄门与宽门The Narrow Gate and The Wide Gate
   马太福音(Matthew) 7: 13-23

1. 窄门的特点  The Characteristic of the Narrow Gate 
a.  唯一的门The Only Gate  (约Jn 10: 9 ) 
b.  古道的门The Old Gate (耶Jer 6: 16)  
c.  狭窄的门 The Narrow Gate (太Mt 7: 13) 
d.  找着的门The Found Gate (太Mt 7: 14) 
e.  苦中有乐的门 The Door to Happiness in Suffering

2.  谨防假先知 Be Alert with the False Prophets 
a.  树与果子 Tree and Fruit (太Mt 7: 15-19)
b.  到那日On that day (太Mt 7: 20-23)

让我们通过耶稣进入窄门，谨防假先知，建造生命，通向永恒！
Let us enter the narrow gate through Jesus, be aware of false prophets, build  life,  and lead to eternity!
