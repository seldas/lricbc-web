# 信息摘要

小儿子与大儿子的比喻
The Parable of the Younger Son and the Older Son
    路加福音 (Luke) 15: 11-32

小儿子 The Younger Brother 
         1) 他的请求 His Request 
         2) 他醒悟过来 He came to his senses(15: 17)
         3) 他起来 He got up (15: 20)
         4) 他被得回 He was found (15: 21-24)

大儿子 The Older Brother 
          1) 在家与离家at home or off home?
          2) 儿子亦或仆人 sonship or servantship?
          3) 有愛还是无愛 Loving or loveless?

父亲 Father
1) 对待小儿子 Treat the younger son
    于是起来，往他父亲那里，相离甚远，他父亲看见，就动了慈心，跑去抱着他的颈项，连连与他亲嘴。
So he got up and went to his father. But while he was still a long way off, his father saw him and was filled with compassion for him; he ran to his son, threw his arms around him and kissed him  (15: 20）

2) 对待大儿子 Treat the older son
     大儿子却生气，不肯进去；他父亲就出来劝他
The older brother became anger and refused to go in. So his father went out and pleased with him  (15: 28）

我是小儿子吗？Am I the younger son？
我是大儿子吗？Am I the older son?
