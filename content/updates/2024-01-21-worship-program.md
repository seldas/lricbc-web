# 信息摘要

主餐礼的意义
									The Significance of The Lord’s Supper

林前1 Cor. 11: 17-34
									主旨Thesis
									主餐礼是与主更近，认罪更深、愛人更真诚的时刻！

1. 要在合一相愛里守主餐 (11: 17-22, 33-34)
									Observe the Lord’s Supper in unity and love

2. 要按主的吩咐吃主餐 (11:23-26)
									Eat the Lord’s Supper according to the Lord’s institution

a. 纪念主 Remember the Lord
									b. 表明主的死 Proclaim the Lord’s death
									c. 显明与主的联合 Demonstrate union with the Lord

3. 要按理领主餐 (11: 27-32)
									Receive the Lord’s Supper in a Worthy Manner

a. 省察自己 Examine yourself
									b. 分辨自己 Judge yourself

结语Conclusion
									愿今日主餐礼彰显圣父的指引、圣子的生命和圣灵的充满！
									May today’s Lord’s Supper reflect the guidance of the Father, the life of the Son, and the filling of the Holy Spirit!
