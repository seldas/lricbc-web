# 信息摘要

逾越节与新年

主旨：我们过蒙神喜悦的新年
Thesis: We celebrate a new year that pleases God

相似点Similarity
1.    最大的节日The biggest holiday (12: 1)
2.    庆祝时间长Many days (12: 15-16) 
3.    不工作No work (12: 16) 
4.    讨债钱Debt collection (12: 35)

相似却不同 Similar but Different Points 
1.    新年与老天爷/ 逾越节与上帝 (12: 1, 11, 12, 14)
Lunar New Year & Heavenly Grandpa / Passover & God  
2.    新年与十二生肖/ 逾越节与羔羊 (12: 3-11）
Lunar New Year & the Twelve Animals/ Passover & the Lamb  
3.    新年与饺子/ 逾越节与无酵饼 (12: 8, 15, 17-19)
Lunar New Year & Dumpling/ Passover & the Unleavened Bread

新年的宣告 New Year Statements:
* 唯耶和华是独一主，我应一生敬畏祂
 Only Jehovah is the only Lord, I should worship Him all my life
* 唯基督是拯救者，我要天天跟随祂
Only Christ is the Savoir, I shall follow Him every day
* 唯圣灵使我成圣，我靠祂必全然得胜！
Only the Holy Spirit sanctifies me, and I can do all things by His power!
