# 信息摘要

书卷和羔羊 The Scroll and The Lamb
启示录 (Rev.elation) 5 : 1-14

主旨: 羔羊配得颂赞,我应全然奉献。
Thesis: The Lamb is worthy of praise; I will give Him all.

• 天上的诗班 The Heaven Choir
1. 我看见书卷 I saw a scroll (5: 1)
2. 我看见天使与长老的对白
I saw a dialogue between an angel and an elder (5: 2-5)
3 .我看见羔羊 I saw a Lamb (5: 6-10)
4. 我看见天使 I saw angels (5: 11-14)

• 完全的福音 A Full Gospel (5: 9-10)
1. 羔羊受死 The death of the Lamb
2. 救赎 Salvation
3. 成圣 Sanctification
4. 得荣耀 Glorification
