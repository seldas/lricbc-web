# 信息摘要

“合神心意的大卫王”信息系列之四
The fourth of sermon series: “The King David after God's own heart”
大卫与米非波设 David & Mephibosheth
撒母耳記下(2 Samuel) 9: 1-3, 11-13 ; 19: 24-30 ; 21:5-7

主旨 Thesis
慈爱的上帝接纳罪人，奇异恩典何等甘甜！
A loving God who accepts sinners, how sweet the amazing grace is!

1. 大卫的恩惠 David’s kindness
(撒下2 Sam 9: 1-13; 弗Eph 2: 4-7)

2. 米非波设的忠诚 Mephibosheth’s loyalty
(撒下2 Sam 19: 24-30; 16:1-4; 提前1 Tim 6:6-8)

3. 米非波设后来的结局 What happened to Mephibosheth later
a. 保全性命Preserve life (21: 5-7)
b. 得着后裔 Receive offspring (9:12; 代上1 Chron 8: 34-35)

反思 Reflection
上帝对我们、我们对上帝、以及在基督里永恒的生命！
God to us, we to God, and the eternal life in Christ!
