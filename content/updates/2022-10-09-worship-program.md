# 信息摘要

“在真道上持守”：希伯来书后四讲
希伯来书 (Hebrews) 4:14-16
坦然无惧来到主面前
Draw Near To The Lord With Confidence

* 两项劝勉  Two Exhortations   
1. 持定所承认的道  Hold on to the faith we confess (来Heb 4:14)
a.  原因：耶稣的职任
Reason: Jesus’ Office 
b.  内容：共同的信
Content: Our profession of faith  
2. 来到主面前  Draw near to the Lord (来Heb 4:15-16)
a.  保障 Assurance
b.  态度 Attitude 
c.  收获 Attainment

结语  Conclusion
愿我们时刻来到施恩宝座前，得享上帝的怜悯与恩惠！
 May we come to the throne of grace for God’s mercy and grace at any time!

*让我们预备心灵守主餐礼
Let us prepare our heart for the Lord’s Supper
