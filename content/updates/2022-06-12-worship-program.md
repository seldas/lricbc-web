# 信息摘要

登山宝训系列——最后一篇

稳固根基The Firm Foundation
马太福音(Matthew) 7: 24-27;
林前(1 Co) 3: 10-15

•    选立根基  To choose a foundation
如何为人生选立根基？How to choose a foundation for your life? 
a.  只有一个  Only one foundation 
b.  它坚固吗?  Is it firm?  
c.   能存到永恒吗?  Will it last to eternity? ——林前1 Co 3: 11

•    上面建造  To build above it 
a. 材料Materials (林前1Co 3: 12)
b. 建造Build—行道do what the word says (雅James 1:22-24)

•    面对冲击  To face challenges (太Mt 7: 25, 27) 
淋descend、冲come、吹blow、撞beat

•    聪明人与无知人  A wise man or a foolish man

结语  Conclusion
愿主保守我们在祂稳固的根基上建造工程，等待天父的验收！
May the Lord keep us to build on His firm foundation, waiting for the Heavenly Father’s approval!
