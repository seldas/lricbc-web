### 信息摘要
### “耶稣基督并他钉十字架”——宣讲《哥林多前书》之十二
“Jesus Christ and Him Crucified”: Preaching on 1 Corinthians (XII)  
### 关于祭偶像的食物（之一）On Food Sacrificed to Idols (I)
### 经文：哥林多前书 (1Cor) 8章—10章
### 读经：林前1 Cor. 8: 1-13 ; 10: 14-33
### 主旨 Thesis
### 我们要在吃喝上当荣神益人！
We should glorify God and benefit others in what we eat and drink!  
### 大纲 Outline
* **1.** 8:1–13 知识与爱心：基督徒对偶像祭物的基本态度
Knowledge and Love: The Basic Attitude of Christians toward Food  
Sacrificed to Idols  
* **2.** 9:1–27 自由与约束：为福音甘心放弃权利
Freedom and Restraint: Willingly Giving Up Rights for the Sake of  
the Gospel  
* **3.** 10:1–13 历史的警戒：以色列人滥用自由的危害
Lessons from History: The Dangers of Israel’s Abuse of Freedom  
* **4.** 10:14–22 与主联合：逃避拜偶像
Union with the Lord: Flee from Idolatry  
* **5.** 10:23–33 自由的原则：一切为荣神益人
The Principle of Christian Freedom: All Things for God’s Glory and  
Others’ Good  
### 背诵经文Memory Scripture: 林前( 1 Cor) 10:31
