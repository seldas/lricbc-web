The Ministry of Jesus 耶穌的宣教
马太福音 (Matthew) 9: 35-38 ; 28: 16-20

• Jesus’ Involvement 耶穌的参与
(太 Mt 9:35; 腓 Php 2: 6-8; 约 Jn 1: 1-3; 启 Rev 19:11-16)

a) Jesus came to teach 耶穌来教导
b) Jesus came to preach 耶穌來传道 (林前 1 Cor 2:9)
c) Jesus came to heal 耶穌来医治
d) Jesus came to save 耶穌来拯救

a) The harvest is plentiful 要收的庄稼多
b) The workers are few 作工的人少
