## Message 信息

**The Altar in the Outer Court / 外院中的祭坛**  
*"God is with us": Preaching on the Tabernacle (VIII) / "神人同住"——宣讲会幕之八*  
*Pastor Li Chunhai (李春海牧师)*

**Thesis 主旨**

Through the altar, we discover the true pathway of divine-human communion — fellowship, love, being drawn to God, and following after Him.

* **Position of the Altar 祭坛的位置** (Exodus 40:29)
* **Details and Their Significance 细节和其意义** (Exodus 27:1–8; Leviticus 6:8–13)
* **A Challenge to Us 对我们的挑战** (Hebrews 13:10–14)

**Conclusion 结语** (Hebrews 13:13)

> "Let us, then, go to him outside the camp, bearing the disgrace he bore."  
> "这样，我们也当出到营外，就了他去，忍受他所受的凌辱。"

*Scripture Memory: Leviticus 6:13 / 背诵经文：利未记 6:13*

> "The fire must be kept burning on the altar continuously; it must not go out."  
> "在坛上必有常常烧着的火，不可熄灭。"
