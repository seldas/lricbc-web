## Message 信息

**The Prayer in Gethsemane / 客西马尼园的祷告**  
*"The Power of the Gospel": Preaching Christ's Suffering and Resurrection (1) / "福音的大能"——宣讲基督的受难与复活（上）*  
*Pastor Li Chunhai (李春海牧师)*

**Thesis 主旨**

The Lord in Gethsemane is calling us to pray today! 在客西马尼园中的主，今天仍呼召我们祈祷！

* The Command to Pray 祷告的命令 (Luke 22:39–40)
* The Example of Prayer 祷告的榜样 (22:41)
* The Content of Prayer 祷告的内容 (22:42)
* More Fervent Prayer 祷告的深入 (22:43–44)
* The Result of Prayer 祷告的果效 (22:45–46; Heb 5:7–10)

**Conclusion 结语**

Rise and Pray! 起来，祷告！

*Scripture Memory: Mark 14:38 / 背诵经文：马可福音 14:38*

> "Watch and pray so that you will not fall into temptation. The spirit is willing, but the flesh is weak."  
> "总要警醒祷告，免得入了迷惑。你们心灵固然愿意，肉体却软弱了。"
