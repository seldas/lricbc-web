### 客西马尼园的祷告 The Prayer in Gethsemane

**读经 Scripture Reading:** Luke (路) 22:39–46; Hebrews (来) 5:7–10
**参考经文 Reference:** Matthew (太) 26:36–46; Mark (可) 14:32–42

### 主旨 Thesis

> The Lord in Gethsemane is calling us to pray today!
> 在客西马尼园中的主，今天仍呼召我们祈祷！

* The Command to Pray 祷告的命令 (Luke 路 22:39–40)
* The Result of Prayer 祷告的果效 (22:45–46; Heb 来 5:7–10)

### Conclusion 结语

> Rise and Pray!
> 起来，祷告！

### Scripture Memory 背诵经文: Mark (马可福音) 14:38

> "Watch and pray so that you will not fall into temptation. The spirit is willing, but the flesh is weak."
> "总要警醒祷告，免得入了迷惑。你们心灵固然愿意，肉体却软弱了。"
