# 信息摘要

神的福音：宣讲罗马书 （十）
			God’s Gospel: Preaching the book of Romans X

成圣之路  The Way to Sanctification
			罗马书 (Romans) 6: 1-23

主旨 Thesis
			向罪死，向神活  
			Die to sin, and live to God

反问 Rhetorical questions (6:1-3)
				浸礼的灵意 The spiritual meaning of baptism (6: 4-10)
				命令 Imperatives (6:11-14)
				反问 Rhetorical questions (6:15-16) 
				从前与现在Past and present (6:17-23)

看自己是“死活人” Consider yourself as a living man who is dead (6:11)
				不容罪做王 Do not let sin reign over you (6:12)
				不把肢体献给罪、要把肢体献给神 Do not offer your parts of body to sin, but offer your parts of body to God (6:13)
				不做罪奴、只做义奴  Do not be a slave of sin, but a slave of righteousness (6:19)

结语 Conclusion
			“生命仅一次，转瞬即逝；唯为基督所成就的，才能永存。” 施达德
			“Only one life, and it’ll soon be past,
			only what’s done for Christ will last.” (C. T. Studd)
