# 信息摘要

讲章系列：神的心意在家庭之一

重回伊甸园 --亚当的家庭
       Return To The Garden of Eden --Adam’s Family
       创世記 (Genesis) 2: 8, 15 ; 3: 4-5 ; 4: 8, 26

伊甸园：喜乐之地  The Garden of Eden: the place of delight or pleasure

•家庭的骗局—撒旦的谎言 (创Ge 3: 4-5)
      Family’s Scam:  Satan’s Lie  
      a) 怀疑神的话  Doubt God’s word 
      b) 像神知善恶  Be like God knowing good and evil

•家庭的目标—重回伊甸园 (创Ge 2: 8, 15) 
      Family’s Goal: Return to the Garden of Eden
      伊甸园中的有与无  There is and there isn’t in it

• 家庭的问题Family’s Issues
      a) 夫妻：甩锅  Husband & Wife: Shirk one’s responsibility (创Ge 3:12-13)
      b) 兄弟：嫉妒Brothers: Jealousy (创Ge 4: 8)

•家庭的出路Way out for Family 
      a) 穿皮衣代替无花果叶子 Wear skin garment instead of fig leaves (创Ge 4:21) 
      b) 求告神Ask for God (创Ge 4:26)

结语Conclusion
   求主使我们的家庭成为乐园！
May the Lord bless our family to be a paradise!
