## Order of Worship — February 8, 2026

## Message 信息

### "Jesus Christ and Him Crucified": Preaching on 1 Corinthians (XXIII) "耶稣基督并他钉十字架"——宣讲《哥林多前书》之二十三

**The Offering for God's People 为神子民奉献** — 1 Cor. 16:1–4; 2 Cor. 9:6–11; 1 Cor. 16:22–24

**Thesis 主旨**

Participate in God's Great Work through Giving and Enjoy the Sweetness of His Blessing
以奉献参与神家圣工，享受神恩甘甜

**The Proposal for the Offering 奉献提议** (1 Cor. 16:1–4)

1. The Need of the Saints 圣徒的需要 (16:1)
2. A Consistent Teaching 一贯教导 (16:1b)
3. A Clear Procedure 具体流程 (16:2–4)

**Generosity Encouraged 勉励乐捐** (2 Cor. 8:1–9:15)

1. The Example of the Macedonian Churches 马其顿众教会的榜样 (8:1–5)
2. Sowing Generously 多种多收 (9:6–7)
3. Promises 应许 (9:8–15)
   * Giver 奉献者
   * Receiver 受惠者

**Conclusion 结语** (1 Cor. 16:22–24)

> "If anyone does not love the Lord — a curse be on him. Come, Lord!"
> "若有人不爱主，这人可诅可咒。主必要来！"

**Scripture Memory 背诵经文: 1 Corinthians 16:22 / 林前16:22**
