### 信息摘要
### 神賜福的日子 The Day Blessed By God
### 哈该书 (Haggai) 2: 10-19
* **1.** 不以神为中心时所有層面都被污秽（该 Hag 2:10-14）
When God is not at the center all aspects are defiled  
* **2.** 以神为中心时必蒙神賜福   (该Hag 2:15-19）
When you center your heart on God, you will surely be blessed by God  
### * 哥林多前书 (1 Cor) 3:11
### “因为那已经立好的根基就是耶稣基督，此外没有人能立别的根基。”
“For no one can lay any foundation other than the one  already laid, which is Jesus Christ.”  
### * 以弗所书 (Eph) 4:12
### “为要成全圣徒，各尽其职，建立基督的身体，”
“ to equip his people for works of service, so that the body of Christ may be built up.”
