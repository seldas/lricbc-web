# 信息摘要

穿白衣的众人
The Great Multitude in White Robes
启示录 (Revelation) 7: 9-17

主旨：让我们体会上帝对万民敬拜的心肠
Thesis:  Let’s long for the Lord’s heart of worship for all peoples

1. 同来敬拜 They worship together (7: 9-12)
• 代表Representatives 
• 独特统与一性Uniqueness and Unity 
• 语言Language

2.  同得救恩 They receive salvation together (7: 13-14)  
 • 同经患难 They experienced the great tribulation
 • 同蒙洁净 They were washed

3.  同蒙祝福 They will be blessed together (7: 15-17)
 A. 侍奉祂Serve Him (7: 15) 
 B. 羔羊牧养The Lamb will shepherd
      a) Presence同在 (7: 15) 
      b) Provision 供应 (7: 16a)
      c) Protection 保护 (7:16b)
      d) Leading 引领 (7: 17)
      e) Comfort 安慰 (7:17)

挑战 Challenge  
Be involved in the Vision and Be a Part of the Vision
