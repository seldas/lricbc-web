禁食的定义  The Definition of Fasting（太Mt 6: 16-18）          
1. 禁食不是绝食而是顺服Fasting is not protest but submission

2. 禁食不是炫耀而是谦卑Fasting is not showing off but humility

1. 认罪悔改  Confess and Repent (拿Jnh 3: 5-10）
2. 开始圣工  Start Ministry (太Mt 4: 1-11)
3  亲近上帝  Be Closer to God (出Ex 34: 28) 
4. 迎接挑战  Face the Challenge (斯Est 4:16） 
5. 开启宣教  Embark on Mission (徒Acts 13: 1)

—突破灵性的瓶颈  Breakthrough the spiritual bottleneck              
—渴慕与神更深的关系Be eager to have a deeper relationship with God
—经历神的大能  Experience God’s power

结语  Conclusion:
求神复兴我们祈祷的生活！
May the Lord revive our prayer life!
