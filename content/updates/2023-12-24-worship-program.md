# 信息摘要

“耶稣带来什么”系列信息之四
									 A Series of Messages: “What Does Jesus Bring?” (4)

耶稣带来真理  Jesus Brings Truth
						约翰福音 (John) 8:1-12, 14, 16, 26, 28, 31-32, 40, 58

主旨 Thesis 
						      律法是借着摩西传的，恩典和真理都是由耶稣基督来的。
						      For the law was given through Moses;
						       grace and truth came through Jesus Christ.

耶稣所作带出真理 What Jesus did to bring truth (约8:1-11) 
							耶稣所言彰显真理 What Jesus said reveals the truth（约8:12-59）

我是世界的光 I am the light of the world (8:12)
							我的见证是真的 My testimony is true (8:14)
							我的判断是真的 My judgment is true (8:16)
							那差我来的是真的 He who sent me is true (8:26)
							我是基督 I am (8:28)
							你们要作我的真门徒 You should be my true disciples (8:31)
							你们必晓得真理 You will know the truth (8:32)
							我讲真理 I tell the truth (8:40, 44-46)
							我就是 I am (8:58)

结语 Conclusion
						 主啊，你有永生之道，我们还归从谁呢？
						 Lord, to whom shall we go? You have the words of eternal life!
