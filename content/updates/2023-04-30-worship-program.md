# 信息摘要

启示录 (Revelation) 21: 1-27
新耶路撒冷 The New Jerusalem

主旨 Thesis
神儿女有荣美的家乡；我们活在大盼望中
God’s children have a glorious hometown; we live in great hope

宝座上的声音 The Voice from the Throne
1. 上帝的同在 God’s presence (21: 3)
2. 天堂的公民The heavenly citizens (21: 7-8, 27)

天堂的样式The Image of Heaven
1. 神的荣耀God’s glory (21: 11, 23; 22: 5)
2. 城是四方的 The city is laid out like a square (21: 16)
3. 城是精金的The city is of pure gold (21: 18)
4. 墙是碧玉的 The wall is of jasper (21: 18)
5. 十二个门Twelve gates (21: 12-13, 21)
6. 十二个根基Twelve foundations (21: 14, 19-20)
7. 没有圣殿 Without temple (21: 22)
8. 城门不关闭Its gates never be shut (21: 25)
9. 生命河The river of life (22: 1-2)

挑战 Challenge
弟兄姐妹，你还惧怕死亡吗？
Brothers and sisters, do you still fear death?
