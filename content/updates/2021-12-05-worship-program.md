# 信息摘要

“失丧与寻回“第一篇

迷羊与失钱的比喻
      The Parables of the Lost Sheep & The Lost Coin 
            (路加福音Luke 15: 1-10)

两个比喻的重点  The Main Points of the Two Parables

谁有……或是…… Suppose one of you… or suppose a woman (15: 4, 8)
	丢失的事实  The Fact of the Lost (15: 4,8）
	寻找的代价  The Cost of Looking For (15: 4, 8)
	庆祝的必要  The Necessity of Celebrating (15: 6, 9）

我是否失丧了？Am I lost?
	我是否愿意去寻找失丧者，直到找回来呢？Am I willing to look for the lost until I find him?
	我是来一同为罪人悔改庆祝吗？Do I come to celebrate together because the sinner repents?
