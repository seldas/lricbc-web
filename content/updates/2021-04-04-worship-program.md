# 信息摘要

“祂不在这里,已经复活了 He is Not Here; He Has Risen”
(马太福音Mt. 28:1-10)

耶稣复活的要点 The Main Points of Jesus’ Resurrection
 1. 寻找钉十字架的耶稣 Looking for Jesus, who was crucified (太 28: 5)
 2. 来看安放主之处 Come and See the Place where He lay (太 28: 6)
 3. 去告诉门徒 Go and Tell Disciples (太 28: 7)

结语 Conclusion
求主将复活的喜乐大大充满每一位弟兄姐妹!
May every one of us be filled with the joy of Jesus’ Resurrection greatly!
