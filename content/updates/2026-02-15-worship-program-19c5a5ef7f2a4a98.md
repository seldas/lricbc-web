## Order of Worship — February 15, 2026

## Scripture Reading 读经

**2 Thessalonians 1:1–12 帖后 1:1–12**

> We pray this so that the name of our Lord Jesus may be glorified in you, and you in him, according to the grace of our God and the Lord Jesus Christ.

## Message 信息摘要

**Instruction and Blessings for the Church / 给教会的劝勉与祝福**

**I. Grace and Peace Come from God the Father and the Lord Jesus Christ**  
恩典与平安从父神和主耶稣基督而来

* **A.** Thanksgiving for His Church — 为祂的教会献上感恩
* **B.** Perseverance and Faith through Persecution and Trials — 在逼迫与患难中持守忍耐与信心
* **C.** God's Righteous Judgement — 神公义的审判
* **D.** Worthy of His Calling — 行事为人配得祂的呼召

**II. Stand Firm in the Word**  
在真道上站立得稳

* **A.** Saved through sanctification and belief in the Truth — 因成圣和对真理的信而得救
* **B.** Hold on the teachings passed on to you — 坚守所领受的教训

**III. The Grace of Discipline**  
管教中的恩典

* **A.** Do not be weary in well-doing — 行善不可丧志
* **B.** The Lord of Peace be with you — 愿赐平安的主与你们同在

## Scripture Memory 背诵经文
