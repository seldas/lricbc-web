# 信息摘要

Psalm 63- Worship in The Wilderness
诗篇第 63篇--在旷野中敬拜

* David and his son Absalom大卫与儿子押沙龙 (2 Samuel 撒下15)

* Psalm 63 is a Psalm of trust 诗篇 63篇是一首信心的诗篇

Worship in The Wildness 在旷野中敬拜

1. Recognize God in the Struggle 在挣扎中认定上帝 (63:1a)
 •  God, You are my God 神阿, 祢是我的神

2. Recognize Your Need in the Struggle 在挣扎中认识你的需要 (63:1-2)
•  Early I will seek God 我要尽早恳切地寻求神
•  Longing for God’s presence 渴慕神的同在

3. The Process During the Struggle 挣扎的过程 (63:3-5)
•   Praise God wherever I am 我在任何环境下赞美神 (腓 4:12-13)

4. The Peace During the Struggle 在挣扎中得平安 (63: 6-8)
•  Experience the presence of God 经历神的同在 (賽 43:2-3a)

5. The Confidence During the Struggle 在挣扎中的信心 (63: 9-11)
•  God will remove my enemies 神会除去我的敵人 (來 10:30)
 •  Rejoice in God  在主里喜乐

結语 Conclusion
Trust God and rejoice, you can worship in the wilderness!
  信靠神, 靠主喜乐, 在旷野中敬拜!
