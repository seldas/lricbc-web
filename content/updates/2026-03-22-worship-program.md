### 信息摘要
### “神人同住”——宣讲会幕之三
“God is with us”: Preaching on the Tabernacle (III)  
### 帐幕 The Tabernacle
### 读经 Scripture Reading：出埃及记 (Exo) 36:8-38
### 参考经文 Reference: 出埃及记 (Exo) 26:1-37
### 主旨 Thesis
### 神的子民按神的指示建造会幕；我们要按神的心意建立基督的身体。
The Lord’s people built the tabernacle according to His instructions; we are to build the body of Christ according to His will.  

> **Scripture:** •    次序：神的命令与人的执行 Order: God’s Instructions and Human Response (出Exo 26:1-37; 36:8-38)

> **Scripture:** •    会幕的结构 The Structure of the Tabernacle (出Exo 36:8-38)
* **1.** 覆盖系统 Coverings (36:8–19)
* **2.** 竖板/架 Upright Frames (36:20–30)
* **3.** 闩/横撑 Crossbars (36:31–34)
* **4.** 内幔 Veil (36:35–36)
* **5.** 门帘 Entrance Curtain(36:37–38)

> **Scripture:** •    我们的侍奉 Our Service (弗Eph 4:16)
### 结语 Conclusion
### 愿主继续引领我们, 在真理的合一与彼此相爱的生命中建造祂的教会。
May the Lord continue to lead us to build His church  
in the unity of truth and in love for one another.  
### 背诵经文 Memory Verse：以弗所书 (Eph) 4:16
### “全身靠他联络得合式, 百节各按各职, 照着各体的功用彼此相助,
### 便叫身体渐渐增长, 在爱中建立自己。”
“ From him the whole body, joined and held together by every  
supporting ligament, grows and builds itself up in love, as each  
part does its work.”
