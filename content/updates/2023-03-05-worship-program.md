# 信息摘要

天上的宝座 The Heavenly Throne
 启示录 (Revelation) 4: 1-11

主旨：管窥天上的荣耀，活出敬拜的人生
 Thesis: Behold the glory of heaven and live a life of worship

1. 宝座 The Throne (4:1-7)
• 宝座：坐宝座者
The throne: one sitting on it
• 宝座周围：二十四长老
Surrounding the throne: twenty-four elders
• 宝座中流出：闪电、声音和雷鸣
 From the throne came flashes of lightning, rumblings, and
 thunder
• 宝座前：a. 七灯；b. 玻璃海
 Before the throne: a) seven lamps; b) a sea of glass
• 宝座中和宝座周围：四活物
 In the center, around the throne: four living creatures

2. 敬拜 Worship (4:8-11)
• 四活物: 颂赞 The four living creature: praise
• 二十四长老: 回应 The twenty-four elders: response

1. 让我们用心灵诚实敬拜主
 Let’s worship the Lord in spirit and in truth
2. 让主永驻我心中宝座
 Let the Lord remain on the throne of my heart forever
