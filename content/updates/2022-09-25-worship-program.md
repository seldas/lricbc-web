# 信息摘要

在真道上建立：希伯来书前四讲之四

要吃干粮 Eat Solid Food     希伯来书 (Hebrews)
5:11-6:3

1.  殷勤聆听Listen to it diligently (5: 11)
2.  习练心窍Train yourself by constant use (5: 14)
3.  离开开端、进入完全 Leave the basic truth and go on to maturity (6:  1-3)
六项基要真理  
Six Fundamental Truths 
     a. 懊悔死行 Repentance from actions that leads to death
     b. 信靠神 Faith in God
     c. 各样洗礼 Instruction about baptisms
     d. 按手之礼 The laying on of hands
     e. 死人复活 Resurrection
     f. 永远审判 Eternal judgement

结语 Conclusion
愿真理的圣灵帮助我们，使我们吃干粮，长大成人，成全祂的旨意！
