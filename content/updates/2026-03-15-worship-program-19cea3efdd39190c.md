## Message Summary 信息摘要

### "God Is with Us": Preaching on the Tabernacle (II)
"神人同住"——宣讲会幕之二

### The Lord's Workers 主的工人

**Scripture Reading 诵读经文:** Exodus (出) 31:1–11; 35:30–36:2; 38:22–23
**Reference 参考经文:** Exodus (出) 35:10; 36:8; 37:1; 39:1; 1 Chronicles (代上) 2:20; 2 Chronicles (代下) 1:5

### Thesis 主旨

> The Lord's heart is on man! (Eph 2:10)
> 主的心意在于人！（弗2:10）

* **I. The Order of Workers in Narrative 在描述中工人的出场次序** (31:1–18; 35:1–36:3)
* **II. Workers 工人们** (31:1–6; 35:10; 35:30–36:2; 38:22–23)
  1. Called by God 被呼召 (31:2, 6; 35:10; 36:2)
  2. Filled with the Spirit and endowed with gifts 圣灵充满与恩赐 (31:3–6; 31:34; 38:23)
  3. Specific tasks 具体工作 (31:7–11)
* **III. Our Service 我们的服侍**
  1. God ___________ workers 上帝___________工人 (Matt 太 4:19)
  2. God ________ workers 上帝________工人 (Acts 徒 1:8; Eph 弗 4:11–12)
  3. God begins his work and will finish it 上帝开工也必完工 (Phil 腓 1:6)

### Conclusion 结语

> Come, walk with the Lord, and work with the Lord. (Songs 2:10–13)
> 来吧！与主同行、与主同工！(歌2:10-13)

### Scripture Memory 背诵经文: Ephesians (以弗所书) 2:10

> "For we are God's workmanship, created in Christ Jesus to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do."
> "我们原是祂的工作，在基督耶稣里造成的，为要叫我们行善，就是神所预备叫我们行的。"
