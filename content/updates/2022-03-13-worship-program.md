# 信息摘要

登山宝训” 讲章系列之八
兴起关怀的心
Having a Caring Heart 
马太福音 (Matthew) 6 :1-4

1.    行善的命令The Command of Doing Good 
 *  座右铭Life Motto (诗Ps 37: 3)
 *  真正的虔诚 The real religion (雅Jas 1:27)

2.    行善的动机The Motive of Doing Good(太Mt 6: 1-4) 
•    施舍与奉献  Charity vs Offering/Tithe
•    荣耀自己还是荣耀上帝？Do you glorify yourself or glorify God? 
•    小善与大善  Small good and big good

3.    行善的果子 The Fruit of Doing Good  (加Gal 6: 9; 雅Jas 2:13)

愿上帝兴起我们关怀的心，使我们努力行善，结出善果，并将荣耀归于天父！
May God raise up our caring hearts so that we may strive to do good, bear good fruit, and bring glory to the Father!
