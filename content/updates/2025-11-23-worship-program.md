### 读经 Scripture Reading
### 西(Col) 4:2 ; 帖前(1 Thess) 5:16-18 ; 罗(Rom) 8:28-39
### 主旨 Thesis
### 要在圣灵里感恩！

> **Scripture:** * 感恩，因为主正在我们中间工作 (罗Rom 8:28)
Give thanks, because the Lord is working among us  

> **Scripture:** * 感恩，因为主已经使我们得荣耀 (罗Rom 8:29-30)
Give thanks, because the Lord has glorified us  

> **Scripture:** * 感恩，因为我们必靠主得胜有余 (罗Rom 8:31-39)
Give thanks, because we shall be more than conquerors through Him  
### 挑战 Challenge
### 主啊，开我的眼睛，让我会凡事感恩！
Lord, open my eyes, so that I can be thankful in everything.
