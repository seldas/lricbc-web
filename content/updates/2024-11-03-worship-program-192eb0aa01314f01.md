### 神的福音：宣讲罗马书 （二十五）
God’s Gospel: Preaching the book of Romans XXV  
### 在愛中彼此接纳 Accept One Another in Love
### 罗马书 (Romans) 14: 1- 15: 7
### 主旨 Thesis
### 在非原则的事上用愛心接纳软弱者。
In non-essentials, we should accept the weak with love.  
### •    接纳的命令The Order of Acceptance (14: 1-12)
* **a.** 食物Food (2-4)
* **b.** 日子Day (5)
* **c.** 凡事皆为主Doing Everything for the Lord (6-9)
* **d.** 审判Judgment (10-12)
### •    不让弟兄跌倒Do not put stumbling block in your brother’s way
(14: 13-21)  
### •    守住个人的信心Keep your faith (14: 22-23)
### 结语 Conclusion
### 你们要彼此接纳，如同基督接纳你们一样，使荣耀归于神！
Accept one another, just as Christ accepted you,  
in order to bring praise to the Lord!
