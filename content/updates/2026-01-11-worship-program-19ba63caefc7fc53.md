## Order of Worship — January 11, 2026

## Message 信息

**"Jesus Christ and Him Crucified": Preaching on 1 Corinthians (XX) / "耶稣基督并他钉十字架"——宣讲《哥林多前书》之二十**

### Seeking to Excel in Building up the Church 多求造就教会

**Thesis 主旨**

Let us maintain a proper balance between the two gifts so that the body of Christ may be built up.
平衡两种恩赐，更好地建立基督的身体。

* Eagerly Desire Spiritual Gifts, Especially Those That Edify (1 Cor. 14:1–11) — 切慕属灵的恩赐，尤其要造就人
* Seek to Excel in Gifts That Build Up the Church (1 Cor. 14:12–19) — 求多造就教会的恩赐
* Be Mature in Your Thinking (1 Cor. 14:21–25) — 在心志上作大人
* Use Spiritual Gifts Properly in the Worship Gathering (1 Cor. 14:26–33) — 在聚会中合宜地运用恩赐

**Application 应用**

1. Do I truly desire spiritual gifts for the glory of God and the edification of others? 我是否真心渴慕属灵的恩赐，为要荣耀神、造就人？
2. Do I genuinely care about the building up of the church, rather than merely my own needs? 我是否真正关心教会是否被建立，而不仅是关心我个人的需要？
3. At present, which spiritual gift(s) most needs to be raised up in our church? 现在我们教会最需要哪些属灵恩赐？
4. Am I willing to offer myself fully and allow God to use me to build up His church according to His will? 我是否愿意把自己完全摆上，让神按祂的心意使用我来造就教会？

**Scripture Memory 背诵经文: 1 Corinthians 14:12 / 林前14:12**

> "So it is with you. Since you are eager to have spiritual gifts, try to excel in gifts that build up the church."
> "你们也是如此，既是切慕属灵的恩赐，就当求多得造就教会的恩赐。"
