# 信息摘要

福杯满溢 My Cup Overflows
(诗篇Psalm 23: 5-6)

1. 福：福从天降
Blessing: Blessing is from above
诗115:15-16 你们蒙了造天地之耶和华的福 ！天，是耶和华的天；地，他却给了世人 。
Psalm 115:15-16 May you be blessed by the Lord, the Maker of heaven and earth. The highest heavens belong to the Lord, but the earth he has given to man.

2. 杯：杯藉主成
Cup: The cup is completed by the Lord
诗116:13 我要举起救恩的杯，称扬耶和华的名。
Psalm 116:13 I will lift up the cup of salvation.

3. 满：圣灵充满
Full: Be filled by the Holy Spirit
弗5:18 不要醉酒，酒能使人放荡；乃要被圣灵充满。
Ephes. 5:18 Do not get drunk on wine, which leads to debauchery. Instead, be filled with the Spirit.

4. 溢：活水江河
Overflowing: the living water
约7: 38 信我的人，就如经上所说:“从他腹中流出活水的江河来”。
John 7:38 Whoever believes in me, as the scripture has said, streams of living water will flow from within him.

愿主赐福我们在新的一年里福杯满溢！
May our cups overflow by His abundance in the new year!

1. 教会奉獻: 请將奉獻放进小礼堂后面的奉献盒子
2. 線上奉獻: 點這裡, Fund: Chinese Missions Group
