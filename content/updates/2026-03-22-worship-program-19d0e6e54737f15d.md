## Message Summary 信息摘要

### "God Is with Us": Preaching on the Tabernacle (III)
"神人同住"——宣讲会幕之三

### The Tabernacle 帐幕

**Scripture Reading 读经:** Exodus (出埃及记) 36:8–38
**Reference 参考经文:** Exodus (出埃及记) 26:1–37

### Thesis 主旨

> The Lord's people built the tabernacle according to His instructions; we are to build the body of Christ according to His will.
> 神的子民按神的指示建造会幕；我们要按神的心意建立基督的身体。

* **Order: God's Instructions and Human Response 次序：神的命令与人的执行** (Exo 出 26:1–37; 36:8–38)
* **The Structure of the Tabernacle 会幕的结构** (Exo 出 36:8–38)
  1. Coverings 覆盖系统 (36:8–19)
  2. Upright Frames 竖板/架 (36:20–30)
  3. Crossbars 闩/横撑 (36:31–34)
  4. Veil 内幔 (36:35–36)
  5. Entrance Curtain 门帘 (36:37–38)
* **Our Service 我们的侍奉** (Eph 弗 4:16)

### Conclusion 结语

> May the Lord continue to lead us to build His church in the unity of truth and in love for one another.
> 愿主继续引领我们，在真理的合一与彼此相爱的生命中建造祂的教会。

### Scripture Memory 背诵经文: Ephesians (以弗所书) 4:16

> "From him the whole body, joined and held together by every supporting ligament, grows and builds itself up in love, as each part does its work."
> "全身靠他联络得合式，百节各按各职，照着各体的功用彼此相助，便叫身体渐渐增长，在爱中建立自己。"
