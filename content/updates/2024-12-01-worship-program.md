### 信息摘要 Sermon Summary
An Attitude of Gratitude  

> **Scripture:** Philippians 4:10-20
The Apostle Paul learned an important lesson - How to be content.  
We see in Paul an attitude of gratitude that we should all have.  
How do we develop an attitude of gratitude?  
* **1.** Resist Comparing Ourselves to Others. v.11
* **2.** Rejoice in what we do have. v. 12-13
Phil. 4:4  
Eccl. 5:10  
* **3.** Release what we have to help others. v. 14-16

> **Scripture:** Acts 20:35
* **4.** Refocus on what is going to last. v. 17-19

> **Scripture:** Matthew 6:19-21
Conclusion  
Are you a slave to what you have? You can't serve two masters.
