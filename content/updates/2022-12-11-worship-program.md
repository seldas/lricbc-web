# 信息摘要

12月圣诞月讲章系列之二

牧羊人的见证The Witness of Shepherd
路加福音 (Luke) 2: 8-2

主旨：主找到平凡的人；平凡的人见证主
Thesis: The Lord finds ordinary people while ordinary people witness the Lord

•    天使向牧羊人显现 The angle appears to the shepherds

2.    主的救恩给他们 The Lord’s salvation is for them (2: 10-12) 
3.    主的诗歌唱给他们听They enjoy the Lord’s song (2: 13-14)

•    牧羊人寻找耶稣 The shepherds look for Jesus 
1.    他们往伯利恒去They go to Bethlehem (2: 15)
2.    他们找到主They find the Lord (2: 16)
3.    他们谈论主They talk about the Lord (2: 17) 
4.    他们赞美主They praise the Lord (2: 20)

这个圣诞节，我要更深遇见主、更愿谈论主、更热情地赞美主！
This Christmas, I will come to the Lord more deeply, talk about the Lord more willingly, and praise the Lord more passionately!
