### 信息摘要
### 给教会的劝勉与祝福
Instruction and Blessings for the Church  

## I. 恩典与平安从父神和主耶稣基督而来 Grace and Peace
come from God the Father and The Lord Jesus Christ  
* **A.** 为祂的教会献上感恩 Thanksgiving for His Church
* **B.** 在逼迫与患难中持守忍耐与信心
Perseverance and Faith through Persecution and Trials  
* **C.** 神公义的审判 God’s Righteous Judgement
* **D.** 行事为人配得祂的呼召 Worthy of His Calling

## II. 在真道上站立得稳 Stand Firm in the Word
* **A.** 因成圣和对真理的信而得救 Saved through sanctification
and belief in the Truth  
* **B.** 坚守所领受的教训 Hold on the teachings passed on to you

## III. 管教中的恩典 The Grace of Discipline
* **A.** 行善不可丧志 Do not be weary in well-doing
* **B.** 愿赐平安的主与你们同在 The Lord of Peace be with you
