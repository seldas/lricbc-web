## Message 信息

**Give Thanks / 祝谢了**  
*The Lord's Supper Series (III) / 主餐系列信息之三*  
*Pastor Li Chunhai (李春海牧师)*

**Scripture Reading 诵读经文:** Matthew 26:26–30  
**Reference 参考经文:** Matthew 14:19, 15:36; Mark 6:41, 8:6–7, 14:22–23; Luke 9:16, 22:17, 19; 1 Corinthians 11:24

**Thesis 主旨**

Because Christ gave thanks, we become a thankful people.  
主已祝谢，我们成为感恩的子民。

**Thanksgiving in the Old Testament 旧约的祝谢**

* Deuteronomy 8:10 申命记8:10
* The Jewish Prayer of Blessing (Berakhah) 犹太人"祝谢祷文"

**The Lord's Thanksgiving 主的祝谢** (Matthew 26:26–27)

* For the Father's provision in creation 为父的供应
* For the Lord's redemption 为主的救赎
* For covenant fellowship with His disciples 为与门徒的团契

**Our Thanksgiving 我们的祝谢**

* In everything 凡事 (Job 1:21)
* For everyone 万人 (1 Timothy 2:1–3)
* With offering the fruit of lips 献嘴唇的果子 (Hebrews 13:15)

**Conclusion 结语**

Let us prepare ourselves with thankful hearts to receive the Lord's Supper.  
让我们以感恩的心预备自己，领受主的圣餐。

*Scripture Memory: Hebrews 13:15 / 背诵经文：希伯来书 13:15*

> "Through Jesus, therefore, let us continually offer to God a sacrifice of praise — the fruit of lips that openly profess his name."  
> "我们应当靠着耶稣，常常以赞美为祭献给神，这就是那承认主名之人咀唇的果子。"
