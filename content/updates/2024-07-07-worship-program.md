# 信息摘要

神的福音：宣讲罗马书 （十一）
			God’s Gospel: Preaching the book of Romans XI

与罪争战 To Battle with Sin 
			罗马书 (Romans) 7: 1-25

主旨 Thesis
			知己知彼，百战不殆
			Know yourself and know the enemy,
			and you can fight a hundred battles with no danger of defeat.

1. 真理：我向律法是死的Truth: I died to the law (7:1-6)
			2. 争战Battle (7:7-25)
			a. 从前的失败 Failure in the Past (7-13)
			b. 现在的冲突 Conflict Now (14-25)
			     (a) 两个律Two Laws (14-24)
			     (b) 得胜Victory (25)

结语 Conclusion
			让我们拿起武器与罪争战! 
			Let us carry our weapon to fight with sin!
