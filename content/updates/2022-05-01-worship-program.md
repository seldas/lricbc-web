# 信息摘要

从但支派所得的启示The Inspiration from the Danites士师记 (Judges) 18: 1, 27-31

故事Story :
1. 但占据拉亿 Danites Settle In Laish
2. 但抢夺偶像 Danites Rob Idols

警戒Warning :
1. 他们分到地却不去得地 They were allotted land but did not take it (士18:1)

2.“安居无虑”的危险 A Danger of Unsuspecting and Secure Situation    (士18:7, 10, 27)

4. 他们得地但没有永恒  They obtain land but do not have eternity (士18:31; 启Rev. 7: 1-8)

结语 Conclusion:
         以但人为借鉴，我们不应随己意而行，倒要专一寻求顺服神，走十字架的道路。
Taking Danites as a warning, we should not follow our own will, but instead seek to obey God and take the path of the cross.
