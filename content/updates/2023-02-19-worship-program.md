# 信息摘要

乃缦得医治Naaman Is Healed
  列王記下(2 Kings) 5: 9-18 ; 路加福音(Luke) 4: 27

主旨：主若吩咐你作一件大事，你岂不作吗？
Thesis: If the Lord had told me to do some great thing, would you not have done it?

*  乃缦与先知  Naaman & the Prophet (王下2 Kings 5: 9-19上)

1. 先知的吩咐 The prophet’s command
 2. 乃缦的理性 Naaman’s reason
 3. 仆人的劝勉 The servant’s rebuke
 4. 乃缦的顺服 Naaman’s obedience
 5. 感恩与认信 Thanksgiving and confession

* 基督与我们 Christ and Us (路Luke 4: 27)

结语 Conclusion
无论大事小事，皆要照主吩咐去行！
No matter how big or small, do what the Lord tells you to do!
