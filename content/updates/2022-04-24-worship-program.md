# 信息摘要

要收的庄稼多，作工的人少
The Harvest is Plentiful, But the Laborers are Few
路Luk 10:1-9

我们被命令要作什么呢？What are we commanded to do?

•    耶稣命令祂的门徒要作的第一个行动就是祷告。
The first action Jesus commands His followers to do is to pray.

•    耶稣接下来的命令是，“你们去吧！”
Jesus next commanded them to, “Go your way.”

•    最后，我们要去传天国的福音，作耶稣的工。
Lastly, we are to go proclaim the Good News of the Kingdom and do the works of Jesus.

我祈祷，当你们顺服我们的大君王时，你们便会有主所赐的喜乐和丰收！
I pray for your joy and a harvest from the Lord as you are obedient to our King (路Luk 10: 17)!
