## Order of Worship — January 18, 2026

## Message 信息

**The Gospel of Resurrection / 复活的福音 (1 Corinthians 15:1–4)**

> "For what I received I passed on to you as of first importance: that Christ died for our sins according to the Scriptures, that he was buried, that he was raised on the third day according to the Scriptures."

**The Appearances after Resurrection / 复活的显现 (1 Corinthians 15:5–11)**

### "Jesus Christ and Him Crucified": Preaching on 1 Corinthians (XXI) / "耶稣基督并他钉十字架"——宣讲《哥林多前书》之二十一

**The Resurrection of Christ (I) / 基督的复活(一)** — 1 Corinthians 15:1–11

**Thesis 主旨: Real resurrection produces real faith / 真实的复活产生真实的信心**

Witnesses of the resurrection:

1. Cephas — 矶法
2. The Twelve Disciples — 十二使徒
3. More than five hundred of the brothers — 五百多弟兄
4. James — 雅各
5. All the apostles — 众使徒
6. Paul — 保罗

**What if there is no resurrection... (1 Corinthians 15:12–19) / 如果没有复活…**

**Challenge 挑战**

Live each day experiencing the resurrection of the Lord! 要每天经历主的复活！

**Scripture Memory 背诵经文: 1 Corinthians 15:3–4 / 林前15:3-4**

> "我当日所领受又传给你们的，第一，就是基督照圣经所说，为我们的罪死了，而且埋葬了，又照圣经所说，第三天复活了。"
> "For what I received I passed on to you as of first importance: that Christ died for our sins according to the Scriptures, that he was buried, and that he was raised on the third day according to the Scriptures."
