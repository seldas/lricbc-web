## Message 信息

**Jesus Is Our High Priest / 耶穌是我们的大祭司**  
*Rev. Wei Rong'en (魏榮恩牧师)*

- God speaks to us through His Son Jesus, our High Priest and Lord (1:1) / 神借着祂的儿子耶稣我们的大祭司和主向我们说话
- Jesus washed away our sins with His perfect sacrifice (1:3) / 耶稣用祂完美的献祭洗净了我们的罪
- He rules as King and Priest forever (1:8) / 祂作王作祭司，掌权到永远
- Do not harden your heart (3:7) / 不要使你的心刚硬
- Unbelief separates us from our hope in Christ (3:19) / 不信使我们与在基督里的盼望隔绝
- Those who have believed will enter God's rest (4:3) / 那些已经相信的人会进入神的安息
- We must hold on to what we believed about Him (4:14) / 我们要持守对祂的信仰
- He saves forever those who obey Him (5:9) / 祂永远拯救那些顺服祂的人
- We must strive to be mature Christians (6:1) / 我们要竭力成为成熟的基督徒
- Do not stop meeting together as the body of Christ (10:25) / 不要停止聚会，正如你们是基督的身体一样
- We are to live a life of faith and persevere with holy lives / 我们要过信心的生活，并持守圣洁的生命
- We are encouraged by the faith of those who have gone before us (11:1–2) / 我们因前辈的信心而得着鼓励
- Jesus expects us to live a life of love (13:1) / 耶穌希望我们活出爱的生命

**Conclusion 结语**

Christ our High Priest and Sacrifice is the author of our salvation.  
基督作为我们的大祭司和祭牲，是我们救恩的源头。
