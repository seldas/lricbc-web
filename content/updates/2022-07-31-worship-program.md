# 信息摘要

讲章系列：神的心意在家庭之四

偏心的害处——以撒的家庭

以撒的家庭  Isaac’s Family 
 1)  故事大纲  Outline of Story
 2)  家庭问题的原因The Reasons of Family Issue 
•    以撒的欲望  Isaac’s personal desire  
•    利百加的诡计Rebecca’s scheme  
•    雅各的欺骗Jacob’s cheating 
•    以扫的违誓Esau’s bleach of promise

对我们的教训  The Teachings for Us 
•    切勿偏心Do not have partiality

We seek to be perfect instead of being balanced

May the Lord guide us to walk in His upright and just way!
