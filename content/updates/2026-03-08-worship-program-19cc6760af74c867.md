## Order of Worship — March 8, 2026

## Call to Worship 受感与回应

**Be Moved and Be Responsive** (Exodus 35:20–29; 36:3–7)

## Our Service 我们的侍奉

> "For the gold work and the silver work, and for all the work to be done by the craftsmen. Now, who is willing to consecrate themselves to the LORD today?"

## Message 信息摘要

**"God is with us": Preaching on the Tabernacle (I) / "神人同住"——宣讲会幕之一**

**Willing Gifts 甘心的礼物**

**Scripture Reading 读经:** Exodus 25:1–9; 35:20–29  
**Reference 参考经文:** Exodus 35:4–9; 36:3–7

**Thesis 主旨**

The Lord desires to dwell with His people, and His people are called to give willingly.  
主愿与人同住，人愿甘心献上！

* An Invitation to Build the Tabernacle (25:8–10) — 建会幕的邀请
* An Order of Bringing Gifts (25:1–7; 35:4–9) — 献礼物的命令

**Challenge 挑战**

> "Now, who is willing to consecrate themselves to the LORD today?" (1 Chronicles 29:5b)  
> "今日有谁乐意将自己献给耶和华呢？"（代上29:5下）

## Scripture Memory 背诵经文

**1 Chronicles 29:5 代上 29:5**

> "金子作金器，银子作银器，并借匠人的手制造一切。今日有谁乐意将自己献给耶和华呢？"
