# 信息摘要

“更深亲近上帝”：诗篇选讲系列之二

“歌颂耶和华的搭救 ”——诗篇 34篇

主旨 Thesis
									我要时时称颂主，传扬主的救恩
									I will praise the Lord all the time and preach His salvation

诗人的赞美 The Poet’s Praise(34: 1-7) 
									1. 感恩的赞美 The Praise of Thanksgiving
									2. 邀请众人一同赞美Asking Everyone to Praise together

传道人的讲道 The Preacher’s Preaching (34: 8-21) 
									1. 邀请 Invitation (8)
									2. 敬畏之道 The Fear of God (9-14)
									3. 神的属性 The Attribute of God (15-18)
									4. 基督弥赛亚 Christ the Messiah (19-22)

结语 Conclusion
									主的救恩是我的诗歌！我要时时称颂主！
									The Lord’s salvation is my song. 
									I will sing to Him all the time.
