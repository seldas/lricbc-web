# 信息摘要

“耶稣带来什么”系列信息之一
						A Series of Messages: “What Does Jesus Bring?” (1)

耶稣带来呼召 Jesus Brings Calling
			       马太福音 (Matthew) 9: 9-13

主旨 Thesis
			 深思呼召，回应呼召  Ponder calling, and respond calling

耶稣呼召马太 Jesus calls Matthew (9: 9-13) 
			1. 呼召的见证The Testimony of Calling (9: 9)
			a. 马太的谦卑Matthew’s Humility
			b. 主的恩慈The Lord’s Grace
			c. 马太的回应Matthew’s Response

2.   呼召的意义 The Meaning of Calling (9: 10-13) 
			a. 不是排斥是接纳 Not rejection, but acceptance  
			b. 不是祭祀是怜恤 Not sacrifice, but mercy
			c. 不是义人是罪人 Not for the righteous, but for sinners

3.   呼召的影响The Impact of Calling
			a. 马太 Matthew
			b.《马太福音》The Book of Matthew
			c. 大使命 The Great Commission (太Mat 28: 16-20)

此时，耶稣正在呼召 Now Jesus is still calling……

挑战 Challenge

			  Will you be a part of this year’s “Candy, Christ, Christmas” evangelism?
