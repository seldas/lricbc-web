# 信息摘要

12月圣诞月讲章系列之三

道成肉身

主旨：道为我生，我为道活
Thesis: The Word was born for me; I live for the Wor

道成了肉身The Word became flesh (1:14上)
•    耶稣是道Jesus is the Word (1:1-2)
•    祂成为人He became a human being (1:14)

住在我们中间Make a tent among us (1:14中)
•    “住”：搭帐篷或住在帐篷里 ( 出33:7 )
Dwell: to pitch or live in a tent ( Exod. 33:7)
•    真光驱散黑暗The true light overcomes darkness (1:4-5, 9）
•    祂住在我们心里He lives in us (1:11-12）

带来恩典和真理full of grace and truth (1:14下)

要以基督的心为
Your attitude should be the same as that of Jesus Christ
