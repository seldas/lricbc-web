## Message 信息

**Be Men of Courage / 要作大丈夫**  
*Pastor Li Chunhai (李春海牧师)*

**Thesis 主旨**

May the Holy Spirit nurture every one of us, so that we can become godly people that please the Lord.  
愿圣灵亲自塑造每个人，都成为合神心意的人。

**Conclusion 结语**

May every family member grow in Christ to become a follower of Christ who is watchful, steadfast in the faith, strong, and full of love!  
愿每位家人都在基督里成长为警醒、守道、刚强、有愛的基督人！

*Scripture Memory: 1 Corinthians 16:13–14 / 背诵经文：哥林多前书16:13-14*

> "Be on your guard; stand firm in the faith; be men of courage; be strong. Do everything in love."  
> "你们务要儆醒，在真道上站立得稳，要作大丈夫，要刚强。凡你们所做的，都要凭着爱心而作。"
