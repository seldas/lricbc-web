## Message 信息

**The Table in the Holy Place / 圣所中的桌子**  
*"God is with us": Preaching on the Tabernacle (VI) / "神人同住"——宣讲会幕之六*  
*Pastor Li Chunhai (李春海牧师)*

**Thesis 主旨**

The Lord is our provider at all times. 神是我们随时的供应。

* The Structure of the Table 桌子的结构 (Exodus 25:23–29)
* Its Details and Significance 物件和意义 — plates 盘子 (Lev 24:5–6, 9); dishes/spoons 调羹 (Num 7:7; Lev 24:7); pitchers/cups 爵 (Num 15:5, 7, 10); bowls/jars 瓶/罐
* The Table & The Lord's Supper 桌子与主餐

**Conclusion 结语**

The Lord is our provider; the Lord is our life!  
主是我们的供应，主就是我们的生命！

*Scripture Memory: Psalm 23:5 / 背诵经文：诗篇 23:5*

> "You prepare a table before me in the presence of my enemies. You anoint my head with oil; my cup overflows."  
> "你在我敌人面前为我摆设筵席；你用油膏了我的头，使我的福杯满溢。"
