> "有人靠车，有人靠马，但我们要提说耶和华的名。"

## Message 信息摘要

**The Priestly Blessing / 祭司的祝福**  
*Numbers 6:22–27 / 民数记 6:22-27*

**Thesis 主旨**

Let us understand the true meaning of blessing, live a blessed life, and become a blessing to others.  
明白祝福的意义，活出祝福的人生，并成为他人的祝福。

**I. The First Blessing: the Lord is willing to bless you and keep you** (6:24; Psalm 127)  
第一祝福：神乐意賜福，保护你（6:24；诗127）

**II. The Second Blessing: the Lord makes His face shine upon you and is gracious to you** (6:25; Matthew 1:23)  
第二祝福：神用臉光照你，賜恩给你（6:25；太1:23）

* "Face" refers to the Lord's presence — "臉" 指神的同在
* Immanuel: God is with us — 以马內利：神人同在（太1:23）

**III. The Third Blessing: the Lord turns His face toward you and gives you peace** (6:26; John 14:27)  
第三祝福：神向你仰臉，賜你平安（6:26；约14:27）

* "Makes His face shine upon" and "turns His face toward" — "用臉光照" 与 "向你仰臉"
* "Grace" and "peace" — "恩惠" 与 "平安"

**Conclusion 结语**

May we receive blessings and become a blessing to others.  
愿我们領受祝福，并成为他人的祝福。

> "Some trust in chariots and some in horses, but we trust in the name of the Lord our God."  
> "有人靠车，有人靠马，但我们要提说耶和华的名。"
