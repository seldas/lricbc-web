## Message 信息

**The Priestly Blessing / 祭司的祝福**  
*Pastor Li Chunhai (李春海牧师)*

**Thesis 主旨**

Let us understand the true meaning of blessing, live a blessed life, and become a blessing to others.  
明白祝福的意义，活出祝福的人生，并成为他人的祝福。

**The First Blessing 第一祝福：神乐意賜福，保护你** (6:24; Psalm 127)

The Lord is willing to bless you and keep you.

**The Second Blessing 第二祝福：神用臉光照你，賜恩给你** (6:25; Matthew 1:23)

The Lord makes His face shine upon you and is gracious to you. "Face" refers to the Lord's presence — 以马内利：神人同在。

**The Third Blessing 第三祝福：神向你仰臉，賜你平安** (6:26; John 14:27)

The Lord turns His face toward you and gives you peace. "Makes His face shine upon" and "turns His face toward" — "恩惠" 与 "平安"。

**Conclusion 结语**

May we receive blessings and become a blessing to others.  
愿我们領受祝福，并成为他人的祝福。
