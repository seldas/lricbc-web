### Memory verse: 诗篇 (Psalm) 20: 7
“ Some trust in chariots and some in horses, but we trust in the name  
of the Lord our God.”

---zh---

### 信息摘要
### 祭司的祝福
The Priestly Blessing  
### 民数记 (Numbers) 6: 22-27
### 主旨 Thesis
### 明白祝福的意义, 活出祝福的人生, 并成为他人的祝福
Let us understand the true meaning of blessing,  
live a blessed life, and become a blessing to others.  
### 第一祝福: 神乐意賜福, 保护你 (6:24; 诗 Psa 127)
The First Blessing: the Lord is willing to bless you and keep you  

> **Scripture:** 第二祝福: 神用臉光照你, 賜恩给你 (6:25; 太 Matt 1: 23)
The second Blessing: The Lord makes His face shine upon you and be  
gracious to you  
•             “臉” 指神的同在 “Face” refers to the Lord’s presence  

> **Scripture:** •             以马內利: 神人同在 Immanuel: God is with us (太 Matt 1:23)

> **Scripture:** 第三祝福: 神向你仰臉, 賜你平安 (6:26; 约 John 14: 27)
The Third Blessing: the Lord turns His face toward you and gives you peace  
•             “用臉光照” 与 ”向你仰臉” “makes His face shine upon” and  
“turn His face toward”  
### •             “恩惠” 与 “平安” “grace” and “peace”
### 结语 Conclusion
### 愿我们領受祝福, 并成为他人的祝福
May we receive blessings and become a blessing to others
