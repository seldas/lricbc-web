# 信息摘要

主旨 Thesis
真实的复活、真实的见证、真实的盼望
A true resurrection, a true testimony, and a true hope

1.    复活的意义 The significance of Christ’s Resurrection 
a. 得救的确据 Assurance of Salvation (15: 1-4)
b. 若主不复活会怎样? If without resurrection, then what? 
   (15: 12-18)
c. 基督初熟的果子Christ the firstfruits (15: 20-23)

2.    复活的见证 The Testimony of Resurrection 
 -- 主带领两个教会合一 the Lord led the two churches to be united

3.    复活的挑战 The Challenges of Resurrection 
a. 不可醉生梦死 Don’t live a life of drinking and dreaming 
    (15: 32-34)
b. 竭力多做主工 Give yourself fully to the work of God (15: 56)

结语 Conclusion
求圣灵充满我们活出复活的人生！
May the Holy Spirit fill us to live a risen life!
