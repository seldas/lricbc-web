# 信息摘要

“更深亲近上帝”：诗篇选讲系列之八
												“Be Closer to God”: Preaching Selections from the Psalms VIII

靠祢有力量 Our Strength Is In You
												  诗篇Psalm 84

主旨Thesis
												 我们在主里力上加力。
												 We gain one strength after another in the Lord.

在赞美中得力量Gain strength from praise (84:1-4)

渴慕神院宇 Yearn for Your courts (1-2)
													住在神殿中 Live in Your house (3-4)

在行走中得力量Gain strength from walk (84:5-8)

越走越有力 The more you walk, the stronger you become (5-7)
													越祷越刚强 The more you pray, the stronger you become (8)

在倚靠中得力量Gain strength from trust (84:9-12)

求告 Ask (9)
													对比 Comparison (10-11)
													依靠 Trust (12)

结语Conclusion
												 在主里越久越甜蜜、越走越蒙福！
												The longer we stay in the Lord, the sweeter it becomes, 
												and the further we go, the more blessed we are!
