# 信息摘要

大卫犯罪David’s Sin
撒母耳記下(2 Samuel) 12: 5-25

主旨 Thesis
人的全然败坏和神的赦免之恩
The total depravity of human and the forgiving grace of God

•    那人就是你！You are the man! (12: 7)

•    先知宣告罪的后果 The prophet declares the consequence of sin
1.    罪是藐视上帝 Sin is to despise God (12: 9-10)
2.    家中有刀剑  Swords in house (12: 10)
3.    暗中行恶、明处受报 Do evil in secret and receive retribution in public (12: 11-12)
4.    罪已得赦；仇敌会亵渎，公义要彰显 Sin is forgiven; the enemy will blaspheme, and justice will be revealed (12: 14)

•    大卫对罪的回应David’s response to sin
——认罪confess (12: 13)、忏悔repent (12: 16; 诗Ps 51) ，敬拜worship (12: 20)

•    上帝的慈爱The unfailing love (12:24-25; 太Mat 1: 6)

结语 Conclusion
  因为祂的怒气不过是转眼之间，祂的恩典乃是一生之久（诗30: 5上）。
  For his anger lasts only a moment, but his favor lasts a lifetime (Ps 30: 5a).
