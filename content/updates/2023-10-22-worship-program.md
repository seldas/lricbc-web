# 信息摘要

Preaching Philippians “Follow Jesus with Joy”
宣讲《腓利比书》“喜乐跟随主”

认识我主基督耶稣为至宝
Knowing Christ Jesus My Lord as Treasure
腓立比书 (Philippians) 3: 1-11
主旨 Thesis
改变，持续被改变，天天被改变
Change, change progressively, change every day

• 如何作信主见证？How to give your testimony of becoming a Christian?

• 保罗的见证 Paul’s testimony（腓Phil 3: 1-11）

1. 前提: 澄清真理 Premise: clarify the truth ( 3: 1-3）
   2. 过去: 靠肉体夸口 Past: put confidence in the flesh (3: 4-6)
   3. 转变 Change (3: 7-8)
      a. 扫罗悔改 Saul’s conversion (徒Acts 9: 1-19)
      b. 看法改变 A change of idea
          (a) 有益与有损 Gain or lose
          (b) 至宝与粪土 Treasure or rubbish
   4. 现在因信活着 Now live in faith (3: 9-11)
       a. 更认识基督 Know Christ more
       b. 效法他的死 Be transformed into His death
       c. 经历他的复活 Experience His resurrection

1. 从不信到信 from unbelieving to believing
2. 事工看法的转变 a change of ministry idea

挑战 Challenge
我们要作见证！我们要被改变、被持续地改变，变得越来越像基督！
We need to bear witness! We are to be changed, continually changed,
to become more and more like Christ!
