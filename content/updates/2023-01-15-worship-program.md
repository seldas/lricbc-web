# 信息摘要

王上1 Kings 18: 16-46
The Battle on Mount Carmel: The Second Sermon of Prophet Elijah 
迦密山之战——以利亚先知系列之二

主旨: 耶和华是神。我们当全心信靠他；我们当为万民得救恳切祈祷 
Thesis: Jehovah is God. We should depend on Him only! We should pray earnestly for the salvation of all nations

1.    迦密山的故事The Story on Mount Carmel

1)    以利亚见亚哈王 Elijah met King Ahab (18: 16-18)
2)    约见迦密山The Meeting on Mount Carmel (18: 19- 24) 
3)    辨别真假Distinguish true from false (18: 25-46) 
        (1)    求火 Ask for fire (18: 25-39)
         a.    巴力的先知The prophets of Baal (18: 26-29)
         b.    以利亚 Elijah (18: 30-38) 
         c.    百姓All the people (18:39) 
         d.    假先知的结局The end of the false prophets (18: 40)
       （2）求雨 Ask for rain (18: 40-46)

2.    它对我们的意义Its meanings to us
1)    专一跟随主  Follow the Lord only 
2)    以祈祷争战  Fight by prayer

结语Conclusion
“义人祈祷所发的力量是大有功效的（雅各书James 5: 16下）
“The prayer of a righteous man is powerful and effective.”
