### 信息摘要
### “耶稣基督并他钉十字架”——宣讲《哥林多前书》之二十
“Jesus Christ and Him Crucified”: Preaching on 1 Corinthians (XX)  
### 多求造就教会Seeking to Excel in Building up the Church
### 主旨 Thesis
### 平衡两种恩赐，更好地建立基督的身体
Let us maintain a proper balance between the two gifts  
so that the body of Christ may be built up.  
### •    切慕属灵的恩赐，尤其要造就人
Eagerly Desire Spiritual Gifts, Especially Those That Edify (14:1–11)  
### •    求多造就教会的恩赐
Seek to Excel in Gifts That Build Up the Church (14:12–19)  
### •    在心志上作大人
Be Mature in Your Thinking (14:21–25)  
### •    在聚会中合宜地运用恩赐
Use Spiritual Gifts Properly in the Worship Gathering (14:26–33)  
### 应用 Application
* **1.** 我是否真心渴慕属灵的恩赐，为要荣耀神、造就人？
Do I truly desire spiritual gifts for the glory of God and the edification of others?  
* **2.** 我是否真正关心教会是否被建立，而不仅是关心我个人的需要？
Do I genuinely care about the building up of the church, rather than merely my own needs?  
* **3.** 现在我们教会最需要哪些属灵恩赐？
At present, which spiritual gift(s) most needs to be raised up in our church?  
* **4.** 我是否愿意把自己完全摆上，让神按祂的心意使用我来造就教会？
Am I willing to offer myself fully and allow God to use me to build up His church according to His will?  

> **Scripture:** 背诵经文 Scripture Memory: 林前1 Cor 14: 12
### “你们也是如此，既是切慕属灵的恩赐，就当求多得造就教会的恩赐。”
“So it is with you. Since you are eager to have spiritual gifts, try to excel  
gifts that build up the church.”
