# 信息摘要

生死皆为主
To Live for Christ, and to Die for Christ
腓立比书 (Philippians) 1: 12-30

经文纲要 Scriptural Outline
1. 逼迫兴旺福音Persecution advances the gospel (1: 12-14)
2. 基督传开就欢喜 Be joyful because Christ is preached (1: 15-19)
3. 生死抉择 A choice between death and life (1: 20-26)
4. 要与福音相称Conduct yourself worthy of the gospel (1: 27-30)

生死抉择 A choice between death and life (1: 20-26)
1. 凡事彰显基督Demonstrate Christ in everything (1: 20)
2. 生与死Life and death (1: 21)
3. 选择生Choose life (1: 22-26)

生死挑战 A challenge to death and life
1. 死亡很近 Death is near
2. 活着很好 Life is good

结语 Conclusion
主啊，求祢助我每日死于旧我、活出新生！
Lord, please help me die to my old self and live a new life every day!
