## Message 信息

**Yearning for Peace 渴望和平**
### Isaiah (以赛亚书) 2:2–4; Acts (使徒行传) 17:26–28

**I. Final Peace Among Nations 各国最终实现和平** (Isaiah 2:2–4)

* Mountain for All 众人的山 (v. 2)
* Identification of Authority 识别权威 (v. 3)
* Freedom from War 免于战争 (v. 4)

**II. Divine Origin of Nations 民族的神圣起源** (Acts 17:26–28)

* Creator's Actions 创造者的作为 (v. 26) — Honoring God 荣耀神
* Humanity's Response 人类的反应 (v. 27)
* Humanity's Awareness 人类的意识 (v. 28)

### Conclusion 结语

A. Promised Peace 承诺的和平
