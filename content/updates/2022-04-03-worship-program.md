# 信息摘要

登山宝训” 讲章系列之十一

不要忧虑 Do Not Worry
       马太福音 (Matthew) 6: 24-34

•    不要忧虑，要抓住重点
Do not worry, but get to the point (6: 25-26)

•    不要忧虑，因为忧虑无济于事
Do not worry because worry does not help at all (6:27)

•    不要忧虑，要相信天父
Do not worry, but trust the Heavenly Father (6: 28-31)
•    不要忧虑，要先求神的国和神的义
Do not worry, but seek first His kingdom and His righteousness (6: 32-33)
•    不要忧虑，要活好今天
Do not worry, live well today (6: 34)

结语Conclusion :
不要忧虑！喜乐的心是良药！清心的人必得见神！
Do not worry! A joyful heart is good medicine! A pure heart will see God!
