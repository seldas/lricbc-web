Please Pray for and Participate in the 2026 Church Vacation Bible School

Jesus said: “Let the little children come to Me, and do not forbid them; for of such is the kingdom of heaven.” (Matthew 19:14); Psalm 78:4 also says: “We will not hide these truths from their children; we will tell the next generation about the glorious deeds of the Lord, about His power and the wonders He has done.”

Vacation Bible School (VBS) is a form of “learning through fun” (寓教于乐pinyin: yù jiào yú lè) outreach ministry to the community. It aims to help children come to know God through various activities such as Bible stories, worship, arts, crafts, and games, and to help them grow joyfully in the Lord.

The gospel is the greatest gift God has given to mankind. Sharing the gospel with children is extremely important for the following reasons:

First, children have a relatively simple and tender heart and are less influenced by worldly corruption, making them more willing to humbly receive the Gospel.

Second, all people have original sin, including children; every person needs the salvation of Jesus Christ.

Third, the earlier the gospel is shared with children, the better.

Just like a field, we cannot wait until weeds have overgrown before we begin to cultivate it. Spiritual warfare is the same: the enemy often sows seeds of jealousy, doubt, and hatred in the hearts of young children. Therefore, we must sow the good seed of the gospel before the enemy does—God’s eternal love, grace, and blessing—into their young hearts, so that they may know the Lord from an early age, come to Him, and follow Him all their lives, and not depart even when they are old.

We thank the Lord that our church has already held three VBS programs. Each time has brought abundant fruit. Many of the participating children have gradually been baptized and become disciples of Christ, and many volunteers have also grown together with the children through service. Those who sow in tears shall reap with joyful shouting. May we continue to sow diligently by the power of the Holy Spirit. May the Lord cause the seeds of the gospel to take root, grow, and bear fruit in the hearts of the children, and may all glory be given to His holy name.

请为2026年教会暑期圣经学校代祷并参与

耶稣说：“让小孩子到我这里来，不要禁止他们，因为在天国的正是这样的人。”（太 19:14）；诗篇78:4也说：“我们不将这些事向他们的子孙隐瞒，要将耶和华的美德和他的能力，并他所行的奇事，述说给后代听。”

暑期圣经学校（Vacation Bible School，简称VBS）是“寓教于乐”（ “learning through fun”）的一种面向社区的外展事工，旨在通过圣经故事、敬拜、艺术、手工艺和游戏等多种活动，引导儿童认识上帝，并在喜乐中成长。

### 福音是上帝赐予人最好的礼物。与儿童分享福音是极其重要的，其原因主要有以下几点：

儿童的心灵较为单纯柔软，尚未受到太多世俗影响，更容易谦卑接受福音。人人都有原罪，儿童也不例外，每个人都需要耶稣基督的救恩。越早向儿童传讲福音越好。

正如田地一样，我们不能等到杂草丛生才开始耕耘，属灵争战同样如此：那恶者常常在孩子幼小的心灵中撒下嫉妒、怀疑和仇恨的种子，因此我们需要在敌人之前，把福音的好种子——神永恒的爱、恩典与祝福——撒在他们幼小的心田中，使他们从小认识主、归向主，并且一生跟随主，到老也不偏离。

感谢主，我们教会已经举行过三次VBS，每一次都有丰富的收获，其中参加的小朋友们也陆续受浸礼作主门徒，许多志愿者也在服侍中与孩子们一同成长。回顾以往教会的VBS：
