# 信息摘要

父亲与儿女 Father & Children
以弗所书 Ephesians 6: 1-4

1. 儿女对父母 : 听从与孝敬 (弗Eph. 6:1-3) Children to parent: obey and honor

2. 父亲对儿女 Father to Children (弗Eph. 6:4)
    (1) 不惹儿女的气  Don’t provoke them 
    (2) 照主的教训和警戒养育他们 Bring them up in the training and instruction of the Lord 
* 成为属灵的父亲 Be a godly father 
          a. 同在 Be with them
          b. 谈话 Talk
          c. 展示 Show them
          d. 教导他们顺从 Teach them to obey
          e. 常说两句话：“对不起”！“我愛你”！Say two words often: “Sorry!” “I love you.”

3. 天父赐予你特别的愛 God’s Exceptional Love for You

耶利米书Jeremiah 31: 3 “古时耶和华向以色列显现，说：我以永远的愛愛你，因此我以慈愛吸引你。”
“The Lord appeared to us in the past, saying: I have loved you with an everlasting love; I have drawn you with loving-kindness.”

線上奉獻: 點這裡, Fund: Chinese Missions Group
	郵寄奉獻: Immanuel Baptist Church.
