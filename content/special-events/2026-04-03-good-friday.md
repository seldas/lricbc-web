#### 诗歌 Song：救主舍命心破碎 He Died of a Broken Heart

1. 你曾否念过十架故事, 耶稣在上舍命  
   主宝血从祂肋旁流下, 罪债为你还清  
   *Have you read the story of the Cross / Where Jesus bled and died / Where your debt was paid by His precious blood / That flowed from His wounded side?*

**副歌 (Chorus):**  
救主为你舍命心破碎, 救主舍命心破碎  
为你为我身悬十架, 救主舍命心破碎  
*He died of a broken heart for you / He died of a broken heart / Upon a tree, for you and me / He died of a broken heart*

2. 你曾否念过救主为你, 戴上荆棘冠冕  
   主祷告 “他们所作无知, 神啊求祢赦免”  
   *Have you read how they placed the crown of thorns / Upon His brow for you / When He prayed “Forgive them, oh, forgive / They know not what they do?”*

3. 你曾否念过主仰望天, 说 “成了”是为你  
   你曾否对主说 “感谢主, 因祢为我舍已”  
   *Have you read that He looked to heaven and said, / “Tis finished?” “Twas for thee!” / Have you ever said “I thank Thee Lord, / For giving Thy life for me?”*

---

**主领 (Leader):** 上帝爱世人，甚至将祂的独生子赐给我们，叫一切信祂的，不至灭亡，反得永生...  
*Leader: For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life...*

**会众 (All):**  
主说：“你们一切干渴的，都当就近水来！没有银钱的，也可以来！你们都来，买了吃。不用银钱，不用价值，也来买酒和奶（赛55:1）。  
*The Lord says, “Come, all you who are thirsty, come to the waters; and you who have no money, come, buy and eat!...” (Isaiah 55: 1).*

#### 见证 Testimony：蔡丽霞 Lixia Cai
盲人基督徒蔡丽霞今年94岁，曾在鼓浪屿三一堂担任司琴七十多年。她一岁时因医生误用药物而致盲，她阿姨抱她去请宋尚节牧师祷告。还没开口，宋牧师就说：“上帝有旨意，她盲眼比眼睛看到更好。”她一生眼盲，但却心眼明亮，忠心侍奉主。

*Cai Lixia, a blind Christian, is now 94 years old. She served as a pianist at Trinity Church on Gulangyu Island for more than seventy years...*
