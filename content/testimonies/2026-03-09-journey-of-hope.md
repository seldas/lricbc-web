The slide titled "A 50 year journey of hope" reminded us that every generation is called to report God’s mighty acts to the next. During the ABCFM 2025 celebration we gathered stories from leaders who have planted and tended ministries for decades. They confessed that, like Lamentations 3:22-23 promises, the Lord is faithful every morning, even when fatigue and uncertainty press hard. That fresh mercy has allowed our families to raise up children as arrows—Psalm 127—who themselves now carry the torch. The banner quote, "They will still bear fruit in old age" (Psalm 92:14), became our refrain as we prayed for senior servants to stay green in ministry and for young people to drink in stories of perseverance.

Because the slide emphasized unity—"different places, same God"—we concluded by blessing every visitor with a charge: keep your life evergreen, let joy and hope be your strength, and let your testimony become part of the cloud that surrounds the church.

那张题为「五十载盼望之旅」的投影片提醒我们，每一代都要向下一代传说神的大能事。2025 年 ABCFM 聚会中，我们分享了许多在华语事工里耕耘数十载的弟兄姊妹。他们亲口见证，正如耶利米哀歌 3:22-23 所说，即便身心疲惫，神的慈悲每早晨都是新的，信实从未断绝。这样，许多家庭才能继续把孩童当作神所赐的箭——正如诗篇 127 所描绘——而这些箭如今也在不同角落传扬盼望。我们以诗篇 92 的话作为祷告：“年老的时候仍要结果子，要满了汁浆而常发青”，求神使年长的仆人依然生机勃勃，也让年轻人能喝进走过风雨的见证。

投影片最后那句「虽然我们来自不同地方，但我们事奉的是同一位上帝」，成了我们祝福的起点：愿生命常青，愿喜乐与盼望成为你的力量，也愿你的故事加入那云彩般的见证，环绕在教会的周围。
